Oreo Milkshake

Designed by Freepik

Description

A delicious and easy-to-make milkshake.

Ingredients

  • 6 Oreo cookies
  • 3 scoops vanilla ice cream
  • 3/4 cup milk

Steps

  1. Blend chocolate sandwich cookies, ice cream, and milk together in a blender until smooth.
  2. Pour into 2 glasses.
